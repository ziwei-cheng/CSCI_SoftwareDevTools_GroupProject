How to Play!

-GAME RULES-

You will load into a 2D top down view of a map. This is your arena!
The camera will be centered on the arena and you'll have to keep track of your character which will be a vehicle you get to edit!
Move your vehicle with the WASD keys to dodge your opponents and projectiles.
You will be competing against your friends for superiority and greatest strength.
The highest scores will be tracked and posted on the leaderboard that you can access through the top menu.

Controls:
W = forward
A = left
S = back
D = right
Mouseclick = Shoot Bullet
