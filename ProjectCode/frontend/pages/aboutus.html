About US

-OUR STORY-


Who are we?
We're a group of students from CU Boulder making a .io game for class.
We're glad you chose to play our game.
This game came about when we had to decide on a project for our Software Development Methods and Tools class.
We decided that a web based game would be the perfect way to utilize our already existent skills and learn some new ones along the way.
Our team Computer Mafia is developing this entire website - frontend and backend - ourselves with a little help from our TA and professors.
We hope you enjoy playing our game and make sure check out the many features we spent learning how to develop in class.
